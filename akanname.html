Akan Name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana,children are given their first name as a day name which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. These names include:

Male

  • Sunday-Kwasi
  • Monday-Kwadwo
  • Tuesday-Kwabena
  • Wednesday-Kwaku
  • Thursday-Yaw
  • Friday-Kofi
  • Saturday-Kwame

Female

  • Sunday-Akosua
  • Monday-Adwoa
  • Tuesday-Abenaa
  • Wednesday-Akua
  • Thursday-Yaa
  • Friday-Afua
  • Saturday-Ama
